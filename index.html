Travel Tips

  • Pack light and smart: Use packing cubes and roll your clothes to save space.
  • Stay connected: Get a local SIM card or portable Wi-Fi device for internet access.
  • Learn basic phrases: Knowing a few words in the local language can go a long way.
  • Stay safe: Keep digital copies of important documents and be aware of your surroundings.
  • Respect local customs: Research cultural norms and be respectful of traditions.
